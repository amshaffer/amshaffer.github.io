About us
GreyHoop is an IT engineering firm focused on the design and build of cutting-edge virtualization and automation solutions.
Our consultants have worked in numerous industries across the country, providing depth and experience. All consultants maintain certifications of our vendor partners, such as Citrix, Microsoft, VMware, Veeam, RES, and more. This ensures your business is being served by engineers with the most up-to-date knowledge required to successfully complete your IT projects.
We strive to provide small and mid-sized businesses with top-quality virtualization consulting services. Some of the products we specialize in are:
  • Citrix XenApp
  • Citrix XenDesktop
  • Citrix NetScaler
  • Citrix Provisioning Server
  • Citrix XenServer
  • Citrix WUEM (Norskale)
  • Citrix App Layering (Unidesk) 
  • VMware vSphere and vCenter
  • Veeam Backup and Replication
  • Microsoft Hyper-V
  • Microsoft Server 2008R2, 2012R2, and 2016
  • Microsoft Remote Desktop Services (RDS)
  • And most related technologies!
